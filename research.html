José-Luis Vilchis-Medina

I'm currently a postdoctoral researcher at ONERA - The French Aerospace Lab in the Information Processing and Systems Department - DTIS. I have an educational background in:

  • PhD in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence)
  • (M.Sc.) Electrical and Automatic Eng.
  • (B.S.) Electronics Eng.

My reseach topics mainly focus on symbolic Artificial Intelligence (AI) such as:

  • Knowledge Representation & Reasoning (KRR)
    • Non-monotonic Reasoning
    • Non-classical Logics
  • Logic Programming
  • Decision Theory
  • Resilience Theory and its Applications
