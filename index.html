José-Luis Vilchis-Medina


📬 To contact me, please send me an email to:
🏢 jl.vilchis_medina@ecole-navale.fr

▒ I'm currently an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the French Naval Academy (École navale), and I do my research at the Naval Academy Research Institute (IRENav EA3634) in the Maritime Information Modeling and Processing Group (MoTIM).

Earlier, I did postdoctoral research at ONERA - The French Aerospace Lab, LIRMM, and Aix-Marseille University. Before this, I completed an educational background that includes a:

  • Ph.D. in Computer Science,  Aix-Marseille Université (2018).
  • (M.Sc.) Electrical and Automatic Engineering,  ENSEEIHT (2015).
  • (B.S.) Electronics Engineering,  UABC (2012).

▒ My research topics mainly focus on symbolic Artificial Intelligence (AI) such as:

  • Knowledge Representation & Reasoning (KRR)
    • Non-monotonic Reasoning
    • Non-classical Logics
  • Logic Programming
  • Decision Theory
  • Resilience Theory, and its applications
